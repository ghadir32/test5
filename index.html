ABOUT ME

Hi, It's Dr Mohammad, and I'm glad You're here

I'm a Licensed Clinical Psychologist. I care deeply about connecting with my clients and helping them feel safe and understood.

I have over 10 years of experience guiding clients through the challenging parts of life. Whether it’s the loss of a loved one, difficult health diagnosis, addiction, sexual orientation, or moving forward after trauma... we can work together to find the way forward.

Life Can be Challenging Sometimes.

Challenges in life are a given, and they can be used to your advantage. Each one is an opportunity for personal growth and self-improvement.
The emotions that one could experience when dealing with a challenging issue may include; sadness, anger, loneliness, resentment, anxiety, stress, fear or even depression.
And I’m happy to meet you exactly where you are - because you and I will work together. We’re a team..

Let's Fix This!

It's important that you get the right support, I’m here to help you navigate life’s challenges, and get the life you deserve.


I specialize in mental and neurological illnesses, a psychoanalyst, psychotherapist, dealing with personal disorders and family orientation.

My goal is to help you feel more connected to yourself and others, and empowered to seek out the life you want.

Ultimately, you will be the one to decide on your own best path. I aim to provide you with a safe, respectful, compassionate, encouraging, and non-judgmental environment where you can explore your options..

As your therapist, I serve as your facilitator. I strive to create an upbeat, supportive environment where you can learn and discover more about yourself, your patterns, and your own deeply-held values. You’ll have the time and space to freely explore the challenges you’re facing. And we’ll work collaboratively to build the coping skills and healthy boundaries you need to get unstuck from the thoughts and behaviors holding you back..

So, What are my services and how can i help you?
